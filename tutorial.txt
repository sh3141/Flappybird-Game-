sup nerds welcome to flappy bird 101 , the coolest game ever !!!!!

you can select 2 modes.
Manual mode where you can play the game yourself and
AI mode where you can watch ai assert dominance and completely beat you in the game

### Manual mode :
press SPACE to make the bird fly upwards
press ESC to pause the game for some reason
(which seriously what do you have in your life that is more important than playing flappy bird ???)
press ESC again to unpause the screen
press QUIT to quit the entire game

### AI mode :
sit back and chill
press F to pay respect to the superior AI and help in the AI takeover
press QUIT to close the game
press any key to return to the main menu
